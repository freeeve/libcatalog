{{- /* Publish the projector's redirects.json to the site root. Of the four artifacts `lcat project` writes into assets/, this is the only one whose consumer is the *host at runtime* rather than this build. catalog.json, facets.json and similar.json are build inputs: the adapter reads them and they are correctly never published. redirects.json has to reach the served tree, or `lcat serve` -- and any host configured to read it -- has no map to serve. Referencing .RelPermalink is what publishes an asset (see baseof.html's wasm reader), and for an asset at assets/redirects.json that permalink is /redirects.json. Assigning it to a variable keeps the URL out of the markup. Absent on a site whose lcat predates the artifact, or whose catalogue has retired nothing: `with` simply renders nothing, and the host serves no map. */ -}} {{- with resources.Get "redirects.json" -}} {{- $noop := .RelPermalink -}} {{- end -}}
